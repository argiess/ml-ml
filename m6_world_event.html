MLBB M6 World Championship

The M6 World Championship, the world's premier Mobile Legends: Bang Bang tournament, is underway. With a record-breaking US$1,000,000 prize pool and the introduction of the Swiss format, this year's event is more exciting than ever. Sixteen teams from around the globe are competing for glory. The tournament features a unique structure, with early rounds consisting of best-of-one matches, followed by best-of-three and best-of-five matches in the later stages. The top teams will advance to the double-elimination knockout stage, where they will battle it out for the championship title.
