Suyou Abilities Rundown

Show no mercy with Suyou in 14.22.

Game Updates | Mlml | 11/22/2024

Transient Immortal

Buff

Tap-casting any skill will put Suyou in his Mortal form, grant him 40 Movement Speed and decreasing the interval between Basic Attacks. Hold-casting any skill will put Suyou in his Immortal form, increasing the damage of Basic Attacks to 125% and granting him 20% Damage Reduction. In this form, for every 20 extra Physical Attack Suyou has, he gains 1% Damage Reduction (up to 40%.)

Blade Surge

Mobility CC

Tap: Suyou throws his weapon in the target direction, dealing 250–330 (+100% Extra Physical Attack) Physical Damage to enemies in its path. Suyou then blinks to the weapon's location, catches it, and performs a slash in the opposite direction, dealing 375–495 (+150% Extra Physical Attack) Physical Damage. Hold: Suyou channels the Immortal's power and charges in the target direction, dealing 250–330 (+100% Extra Physical Attack) Physical Damage to enemies along the path. When hitting an enemy hero, he stops and stuns enemies in a rectangular area in front for 0.6 seconds. The charge increase distance increases with the hold time. Suyou can be controlled while charging, but the skill cannot be interrupted.

Soul Sever

AOE Burst

Tap: Suyou performs a sweeping attack with his weapon, dealing [300–492 (+200% Extra Physical Attack) (+target's 20% Lost HP) Physical Damage] to enemies in a fan-shaped area. Hold: Suyou channels the Immortal's power to perform 3 cleaves in a fan-shaped area. The first two strikes deal 250–410 (+80% Extra Physical Attack) Physical Damage each, while the final strike deals 400–656 (+128% Extra Physical Attack) Physical Damage and recovers 150–230 (+50% Extra Physical Attack) HP for himself.

Evil Queller

Damage Debuff

Tap: Suyou glides backward and swings his weapon in the target direction, dealing 420–580 (+120% Extra Physical Attack) Physical Damage to enemies in a rectangular area and on his path back ward, and reducing their Movement Speed by 30% for 1.5 seconds. Hold: Suyou uses the Immortal's power to shoot a deadly arrow in the target direction, dealing 580–800 (+165% Extra Physical Attack) Physical Damage to the target hit. The range increases with the hold time. Suyou can be controlled while charging, but the skill cannot be interrupted.
